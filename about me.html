About Me

Hi, I’m Maya, the founder and owner of Crown. I’m a Cloud Engineer, and I live in Portland, OR. I also serve in the United States military. Originally from Iran, I’ve lived in Turkey, which is why I speak Turkish, in addition to English and Farsi. I moved to the U.S. almost 10 years ago.

The idea behind Crown is to help children in need. I had a very challenging childhood, and I know how hard it can be to see dreams shattered. This inspired me to create Crown, where 1% of each crown sold goes toward supporting children in need.

In addition, I am passionate about helping animals. A heartbreaking experience with my own pet, who I couldn’t afford to treat while in the hospital, inspired me to make a promise to myself. I donate 1% of each crown sold to help people who can’t afford the medical care their pets need.

Thank you for supporting Crown, a brand built on love, empathy, and giving back.
